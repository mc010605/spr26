How We Got Here

2022

The project launched as a simple digital journal, documenting a personal 50-pound weight loss journey with raw photography and weekly milestones. This was where my passion for fitness began.

2023

We introduced "Gear Lab" reviews, testing fitness trackers and kitchen tools to find products that actually support a sustainable lifestyle. Doing these reviews exposed me to so many products that I now have a refined list of actually useful products.

2024

The team expanded to include nutritional and media specialists, shifting toward a magazine-style format with high-fidelity video content. I felt that this was necessary so that my voice could reach a larger audience.

2025

Our test kitchen was officially established, allowing us to rigorously test and approve every recipe before it reaches your plate. I personally have experienced the horror of eating high protein but disgusting foods. That’s why I wanted to establish this test kitchen, so that I could make delicious, yet macro friendly foods.
