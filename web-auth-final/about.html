How We Got Here

2022

The project launched as a simple digital journal. Initially, it served as a personal archive documenting a 50-pound weight loss journey, featuring raw photography and weekly logs of milestones.

2023

We introduced "Gear Lab" reviews. The site transitioned into a utility resource, testing fitness trackers and kitchen tools to see which products actually supported a sustainable lifestyle.

2024

The team expanded to include professional perspectives. By bringing in specialists in nutrition and media, we shifted toward a magazine-style format with high-fidelity video reviews.

2025

Our test kitchen was officially established. This allowed us to rigorously test recipes multiple times, ensuring every meal plan is both nutritionally sound and chef-approved.
