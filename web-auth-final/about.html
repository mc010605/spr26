How We Got Here

2022

The project launched as a simple digital journal. Initially, it served as a personal archive of one individual’s attempt to reclaim their health, featuring raw photography and weekly logs of weight loss milestones.

2023

We introduced the "Gear Lab" reviews. The site transitioned from a personal blog into a utility resource, testing fitness trackers and kitchen tools to see which products actually supported a healthy lifestyle.

2024

The team expanded to include professional perspectives. By bringing in specialists in nutrition and visual media, we shifted toward a magazine-style format with high-fidelity video reviews and science-backed guides.

2025

Our test kitchen was officially established. This allowed us to rigorously test recipes three times before publication, ensuring that every meal plan we recommend is both nutritionally sound and chef-approved.

2026

Today, we continue to grow as a global community. Our mission remains focused on providing a filter-free look at the science and sweat behind weight loss, prioritizing data and honesty over industry noise.
