How We Got Here

2022

The project launched as a simple digital journal, documenting a personal 50-pound weight loss journey with raw photography and weekly milestones.

2023

We introduced "Gear Lab" reviews, testing fitness trackers and kitchen tools to find products that actually support a sustainable lifestyle.

2024

The team expanded to include nutritional and media specialists, shifting toward a magazine-style format with high-fidelity video content.

2025

Our test kitchen was officially established, allowing us to rigorously test and approve every recipe before it reaches your plate.
